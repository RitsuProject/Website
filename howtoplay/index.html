How to Play

Learn how to play the game!

Start the game.

When executing this command, you will start a game with the default settings, which includes 3 rounds of 30 seconds with random themes.

Now it's time to guess!

You now need to type in the chat in less than 30 seconds what is the opening / ending anime that the bot is playing, if you get it right, the bot will delete your message and let you know you got it right, if not, she will ignore it.

After all the rounds are over.

When all the rounds are over, whoever guesses the most animes wins, if they tie, the bot will choose one of the people.
